Gear recommendations for basketball, badminton, running, and more — what to buy, what to skip, and what the regulars actually use.

🏀 Basketball Club

NIT Agartala has an active basketball scene. Here's what you need to get started.

Basketball · Footwear

Nivia Combat 2.0 Basketball Shoes

If you're a complete beginner, start here — this is the sweet spot of price and performance. For more serious players you can go higher, but as they say about going max: "Sky is the Limit."

Basketball · Apparel

Sports Shorts

Essential for playing, but if you already own something similar there's no need to buy. Any comfortable, breathable sports short works fine on the court.

Basketball · Apparel

Sports Jersey

Essential. Any sports jersey is fine — the key thing is that it doesn't absorb and hold sweat. Look for polyester/mesh fabric that releases moisture quickly. Any brand works.

Any breathable jersey works
Basketball · Accessories

Sports Socks (Pack)

Any sports socks work — just make sure you have at least 3 pairs. Sounds trivial, but you'll thank yourself later. Having enough pairs means you're never stuck with wet socks before a game.

Basketball · Equipment

Nivia Basketball

Not required — the college already provides basketballs. Listed here in case you want your own. This one lasts about 6 months of regular use. Not the most durable option but gets the job done.
