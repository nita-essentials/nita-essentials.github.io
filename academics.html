Useful Online Resources

Consistently useful throughout your degree, regardless of branch.

takeuforward.org - Striver's A2Z Sheet

Don't roam around looking for resources. Only resource you need to learn Data Structures and Algorithms. Stick to it and you'll be well-prepared for placements and CP{Competitive Programming}.

Visit Striver's A2Z Sheet →
🎓

NPTEL

Free lectures from IIT and IISc professors. Excellent for every core subject. Many professors at NIT Agartala follow NPTEL syllabi closely.

Visit NPTEL →

Buddy4Study - Scholarships

Platform where you can find information about various scholarships offered by government, private organizations and institutions.

Apply as soon as possible.

Tools & Equipment

Physical tools required for coursework. Check with your seniors before buying.

Calculator

Casio FX-82MS Scientific Calculator

- Required mainly during exams.
- best budget scientific calculator
- does everything you need

You can also borrow from your seniors before buying a new one.

View on Amazon ↗
Drawing

Drafter (Mini Drafter)

Whether you need this depends heavily on your branch and teacher. Talk to a senior from your branch before buying. Mostly needed for Engineering Graphics / Drawing classes.

View on Amazon ↗
Drawing

Drawing Board

Used alongside the drafter for Engineering Graphics. Ask a senior in your branch, you may be able to borrow one or get a second-hand board at a fraction of the price.

Check with seniors first
